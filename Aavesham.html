CINEPHILE


Aavesham

Santhan, a 10th-grader who is constantly bullied at school. He seeks revenge and finds an unlikely ally in Ranga, a local gangster. Together with his friends, Santhan and Ranga embark on a path that leads them into a world of crime and violence. The film explores themes of bullying, revenge, and the destructive power of seeking validation through dangerous means.

Genre : Action

Language : Malayalam

Cast & Crew

Fahadh Faasil, Hipzster, Mithun Jai Shankar, directred by Jithu Madhavan
