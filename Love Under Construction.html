Love Under Construction

"Love Under Construction" follows Vinod, a Dubai-based expatriate, who's building a home in Kerala for his parents, driven by a desire to provide them with a stable life. He finds love with Gouri, a local girl, and their relationship blossoms amidst the construction of his dream house. However, Vinod's life takes a turn when he faces job loss, disrupting his plans and introducing financial challenges. Gouri encourages him to consider migrating to Canada together, presenting him with a new path. The series explores Vinod and Gouri's journey as they navigate their love, personal ambitions, and family relationships while facing setbacks and making crucial decisions about their future.

Genre : Comedy

Language :Malayalam

Cast & Crew
