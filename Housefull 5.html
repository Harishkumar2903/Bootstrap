Housefull 5

"Housefull 5" follows the comedic adventures of a group of friends who find themselves entangled in a hilarious series of mishaps and mistaken identities. As the friends, each harboring their own secret desires and ambitions, embark on a journey that takes them from a cruise ship to the streets of Mumbai. Along the way, they encounter a colorful array of characters, including a flamboyant Bollywood star, a mysterious heiress, and a group of eccentric villains. As the friends navigate a world filled with chaos and confusion, they discover unexpected friendships, face romantic complications, and unravel a web of hidden secrets.

Genre : Comedy, Romance

Language : Hindi

Cast & Crew

Akshay Kumar, Riteish Deshmukh, Abhishek Bachchan, directred by Tarun Mansukhani
