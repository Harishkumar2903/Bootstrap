Shin Chan

English language version based on the Japanese series Shinchan (1992) following the adventures of a 5 year old boy named Shin. It follows the antics of the five-year-old Shinnosuke "Shin" Nohara and his family and friends, primarily set in the town of Kasukabe, Saitama Prefecture, Japan. The series is known for its comedic and sometimes absurd portrayals of everyday life, featuring Shin's mischievous behavior and his fascination with adults and beauty.
