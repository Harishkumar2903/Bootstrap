Andhaghaaram

"Andhaghaaram" follows three individuals – Vinod, a disillusioned cricketer; Selvam, a visually challenged librarian with occult connections; and Dr. Indran, a psychiatrist recovering from a violent attack – whose lives become entangled in a supernatural thriller. The film explores themes of guilt, grief, and the power of the unseen, weaving a narrative that hints at a larger, earth-shattering secret. While the movie presents a slow-burn suspense, it relies heavily on mood and atmosphere, with the narrative threads not fully revealed until the final moments.

Genre :Horror

Language : Tamil

Cast & Crew

Vinoth Kishan, Arjun Das, Pooja Ramachandran, directred by V. Vignarajan
