Squid Game 2

"Squid Game" season 2 follows Seong Gi-hun, four years after his win in the deadly game, as he decides against moving to the US and instead chooses to return to the game with a new purpose. He re-enters the mysterious survival game, where a new group of participants compete for the 45.6 billion won prize. This season sees the return of Detective Hwang Jun-ho, who is still determined to uncover the truth behind the Squid Game.

Genre : Thriller

Language : English

Cast & Crew
